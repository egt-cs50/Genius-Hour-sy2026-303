The first blog

march 2026

You should have already read the "about me" section on the homepage. If not: hi, I'm Gabriel. This is my blog. And I'm Middle schooler who spends too much time on a computer. I play Minecraft, do random stuff, and occasionally do things that make my computer explode.

Passion

Now, why am I doing this website as a project for genius hour? I could have done literally anything else. I could have built a robot, made a game, learned to do something new. But I wanted to make a website. I'm not entirely sure what this'll be. Probably a mix of things I'm working on, stuff I figured out after way too long, and the occasional project that actually works without breaking everything.

I keep running into problems where the answer exists somewhere on the internet, but it's buried in a three-year-old Reddit thread or a forum post where the accepted answer is "nvm fixed it." Writing things down here means at minimum I can find them later.

Materials

A computer and some common sense. And also a lot of trial and error, and probably a lot of help from the internet. Thats it, I guess I may be a tad bit too lazy

This month I'll probably just be making this site better, add some random projects, and probably struggle to make a blog post that isn't just "I did a thing, it was cool, here' s a screenshot." But we'll see how it goes. And a struggle to make a mincraft mod too, oh well.

Anyway. Post one done. Thanks for reading

- Gabriel
